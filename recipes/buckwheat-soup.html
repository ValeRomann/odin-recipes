Homemade Buckwheat Soup

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Onion/li>
  • Carrot
  • Potato
  • Buckwheat
  • Salt
  • Spices
  • Sunflower oil

Steps

  1. Place the chicken in water, bring to a boil, then remove and chop.
  2. ADrain the chicken and refill a clean saucepan with water. Add the chopped chicken to the water.
  3. Peel the vegetables and chop the potatoes. When the water boils, add the potatoes.
  4. Chop the onion, place a frying pan with a little sunflower oil over low heat, add the chopped onion, and grate the carrots. When the onion turns golden, add the carrots. Stir occasionally until the carrots soften.
  5. Pour the fried onions and carrots into the broth, season with salt and spices to taste.
  6. Add the buckwheat and simmer for 15 minutes.
